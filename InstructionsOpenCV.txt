Following are the instructions to use GREEN-CAP to draw diagrams and graphs:

1. Cursor is denoted by pin-point NAVYBLUE circle dot.
2. Press 'x' to start or pause drawing in between.
3. Press 'g' to start drawing with green color.
4. Press 'r' to start drawing with red color.
5. Press 'b' to start drawing with blue color.
6. Press 's' to save and paste on editor window.
7. Press 'q' to quit drawing.
